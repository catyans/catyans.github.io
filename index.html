About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Peking University's School of Computer Science, with a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University (GPA: 3.93/4.0). My research focuses on AI agents design, automated workflow optimization, distributed machine learning, and high-performance computing systems.

Currently, I'm focusing on developing AI agents for data acquisition and analysis, building intelligent systems for automated workflow optimization. Previously, I worked on post-training for domain-specific large models, including SFT and RLHF for medical applications.
