I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the School of Computer Science, Peking University.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University in 2022 (GPA: 3.93/4.0), advised by Prof. Dan Li and Prof. Jianping Wu. Before that, I obtained double degrees in Computer Science (B.Sc.) and Economics (B.A.) from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2017, ranking 1st in both majors.

My research interests span AI agent systems, large language model optimization, distributed machine learning, and high-performance network systems. Currently I am working on building intelligent agents for automated data acquisition and analysis, with an emphasis on trustworthiness and traceability.

Selected Projects

AI Agent for Data Acquisition and Analysis 2023 – Present
Building intelligent agents for automated data acquisition and analysis across vertical domains. Emphasis on trustworthy, traceable data pipelines and end-to-end workflow optimization.
Medical LLM Post-Training 2023 – 2024
Post-training optimization for medical-domain large language models via Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) and Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF), improving clinical accuracy and safety.
Large Model Quantization 2023
Hessian-based weight quantization and high-dimensional subspace compression for efficient LLM inference. Achieved ~30% model compression with negligible accuracy loss.
DaVinci: Unified Sketch for Set Measurements 2022 – 2024
A unified sketch data structure supporting 9 types of set measurement tasks with reduced hash collisions. 90% memory savings and 40× throughput improvement. Published at ICDE 2025.
Elixir: Hybrid Flow Table Management 2019 – 2022
Hybrid hardware/software flow table management for NFV gateways. 50% resource reduction and 97% latency reduction. Published at NSDI 2022.
BML: Distributed ML Gradient Synchronization 2017 – 2018
Multidimensional ring topology for gradient synchronization in distributed training. 25–50% sync time reduction and 56.4% end-to-end training acceleration. Published at NeurIPS 2018.
